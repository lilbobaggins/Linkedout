Community Guidelines

Help us maintain a trustworthy platform for honest company reviews

🛡️ Review Authenticity

  • ✓ Only post reviews based on your genuine experience.
  • ✓ Reviews should be about interviews or actual employment.
  • ✗ False or fabricated reviews will be removed; accounts may be restricted.
  • ✗ Don’t impersonate others or post on someone’s behalf.

🔍 Spam Prevention

  • ✓ One review per company per experience.
  • ✓ Focus on specific, helpful details.
  • ✗ Repetitive or coordinated review campaigns are prohibited.

📝 Content Standards

  • ✓ Be constructive and professional.
  • ✗ No personal attacks or doxxing.
  • ✗ Don’t share confidential info.
  • ✗ Avoid profanity or discriminatory language.
